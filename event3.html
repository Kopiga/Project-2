3 South Indian musical events to be held in Jaffna and Colombo

Oct 15, 2014 (Src:www.culturaldept.gov.lk)

A number of steps have been taken by the Ministry of Culture and the Arts to uplift the national unity in terms of the observations made by the Committee on Lessons Learned.


As such plans are under way to hold a musical event and a workshop known as “Hansadhwani” with the participation of veteran Indian musicians including Professor Unni Krishnan with a view to impart knowledge on North Indian music and entertainment to Sinhala, Tamil and Muslim communities in our country. This program is jointly organized by Ministry of Culture and the Arts and the Foreign Employment Bureaue.


Accordingly it is envisaged to hold “Hansadhwani” musical show on 04th October 2014 at 6.30 p.m. at Manikka Vineyagar Hall at Wellawatha and a Voice Modulation Programe on 07th October 2014 at 10.00 a.m. at Hindu College Jaffna . A “Hansadhwani” show is also to be held in Jaffna on the same day at 6.00 p.m. at Illangei Bennan Arts College, Jaffna. The final show of “Hansadhwani” is to be held on 10th October at 7.00 p.m. at the Main Hall of the Royal College , Colombo 07.


Professor Unni krisnan , Ms.Manesha Sbramanium, Shahanans Khan and Suban Khan will participate in these events.
